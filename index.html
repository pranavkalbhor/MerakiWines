ABOUT MERAKI WINERY

Meraki Wine Company is committed to finding high quality wines and spirits; to discovering accoutrements which enhance the appreciation of these distinctive and authentic tastes. We maintain the highest level of expertise and professionalism in order to market and sell them to food and wine purveyors. We are responsible for shipping, storing, and delivering these products in a manner that preserves their integrity. To evolve as one of the best producer of quality red wine which is consumed for the consumer contentment and to create local opportunity, growth and impact in every community and country around the world as adapting a wide cultural perspective.

The winery provides good vacationing grounds for food and wine lovers. Known for its practices of fine winemaking and harvesting the best quality of grapes across the Nashik region the wines from Meraki are sure to make for an unforgettable experience.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Opening hours: everyday from 9am to 5pm.
